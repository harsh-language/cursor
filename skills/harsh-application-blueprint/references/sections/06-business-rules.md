# Section 6: Business Rules

**Key:** `business_rules`

## Purpose

Defines the logic governing system behavior.

## Questions

- What conditions must be enforced?
- What validations exist?
- What permissions exist?
- What restrictions exist?

## Required output

- Validation rules
- Access rules
- Calculation rules
- Operational constraints

## Minimum bar for `complete`

Rules are stated as enforceable conditions (not intentions). Calculations include inputs, outputs, and edge behavior.

## Compound Engineering skill

`ce-brainstorm` in Phase 3 (review-only) to stress-test rules and edge cases.
