# Section 1: Product Overview

**Key:** `product_overview`

## Purpose

Defines what is being built and why it should exist.

## Questions (ask until answered)

- What is the product?
- Who is it for?
- What problem does it solve?
- Why does this problem matter?
- What outcome should users achieve?
- What outcome should the business achieve?

## Required output

- Product summary (one paragraph)
- Target users
- Core value proposition
- Success criteria (user + business)

## Minimum bar for `complete`

All six questions have user-confirmed answers. Success criteria are measurable or explicitly marked as qualitative with how they will be judged.

## Compound Engineering skill

`ce-strategy` when goals, users, or metrics are unclear.
